Panda redesign study / Huashu pass
ASSUMPTIONS

Generated from the product brief plus current repo tokens.

Panda becomes an instrument panel, not an IDE skin.

SOURCE TOKENS

Warm paper, charcoal dark mode, amber primary, sharp zero-radius panels, mono metadata, dot grid.

The redesign keeps Panda's trust model: one active project, one active thread, visible plan, visible proof, visible changed work. The upgrade is graphical sophistication through stronger information architecture, denser status composition, editorial typography, and a more deliberate split between public clarity and owner-only operational depth.

Narrative Role

Public pages explain the promise. Workbench pages prove the product can be operated under pressure.

Viewer Distance

Desktop workbench targets 10-60 cm inspection. Public pages support scan-first reading at laptop distance.

Visual Temperature

Calm, austere, slightly warmer than common developer tools. Amber signals action, not decoration.

Capacity

High density is allowed only when every datum supports control, recovery, proof, or review.

What Changes

Mode, model, runtime, plan, proof, and diff become one readable operational language.

What Stays

Sharp corners, mono controls, warm neutral palette, explicit approval, bounded summaries.

What Is Removed

Generic AI drama, mascot behavior, dashboard filler, fake metrics, soft SaaS chrome.

Primary Risk

Too much density. The system needs strict progressive disclosure and mobile focus states.
